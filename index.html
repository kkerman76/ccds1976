Jeffrey W. Harrison '76
Award-winning poet · Author of six collections
▼

Jeff is an award-winning poet and author of six books including Between Lakes (2020), Into Daylight (2014, Dorset Prize), and The Singing Underneath (1987, National Poetry Series). He has received Guggenheim and NEA fellowships and appeared in The New Yorker, The Paris Review, and Best American Poetry. He lives in Massachusetts and remains a loyal CCDS supporter.
